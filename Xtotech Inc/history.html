History of Xtotech Nigeria

Founding (2015)

Xtotech Nigeria was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate tech enthusiasts who recognized the growing demand for innovative technology solutions in Nigeria. The company started as a small software development firm in Lagos, focusing on creating custom software for local businesses.

Early Growth (2016-2018)

In its early years, Xtotech Nigeria quickly gained a reputation for delivering high-quality software solutions. By 2017, the company expanded its services to include mobile application development, responding to the rising smartphone penetration in the country. During this period, Xtotech secured several key partnerships with small and medium-sized enterprises, which helped to establish its foothold in the market.

Expansion of Services (2019)

In 2019, Xtotech Nigeria diversified its offerings by launching an IT consultancy division. This allowed the company to provide strategic IT planning and infrastructure implementation services, addressing the needs of larger organizations. The same year, Xtotech also ventured into digital marketing, offering SEO and social media management services to help businesses improve their online presence.

Focus on Cybersecurity (2020)

With the increasing threat of cyberattacks, Xtotech Nigeria recognized the need for robust cybersecurity solutions. In 2020, the company launched a dedicated cybersecurity division, providing risk assessments, security audits, and employee training. This move positioned Xtotech as a trusted partner for organizations looking to safeguard their digital assets.

Cloud Solutions and Remote Work (2021)

In response to the global shift towards remote work, Xtotech Nigeria expanded its services to include cloud solutions. The company offered cloud storage, migration services, and managed cloud support, enabling businesses to adapt to the new normal. This diversification further solidified Xtotech’s role as a comprehensive technology provider.

Recognition and Awards (2022)

By 2022, Xtotech Nigeria had received several industry awards for its innovative solutions and commitment to customer service. The company was recognized as one of the leading tech firms in Nigeria, attracting attention from both local and international clients.

Looking to the Future (2023 and Beyond)

As of 2023, Xtotech Nigeria continues to innovate, exploring emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance its service offerings. The company remains committed to empowering businesses across various sectors, striving to become a cornerstone of Nigeria’s tech ecosystem.
